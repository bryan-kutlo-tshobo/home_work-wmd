Developing Botswana's Finest Strikers

Strikers Elite Academy is revolutionizing football development in Botswana. Founded in 2015, we've established ourselves as the premier destination for aspiring strikers, with a proven track record of developing players who compete at national and international levels.

Our unique methodology combines European training techniques with an understanding of African football dynamics, creating complete strikers who excel in any system.

  • Individualized technical development
  • Tactical intelligence training
  • Physical conditioning programs
  • Mental toughness coaching
