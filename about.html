Our Story

From Township Dreams to Global Football

Eeman

Founder & Head Coach

Developing Botswana's Finest Strikers

Strikers Elite Academy, based in Gaborone West, Botswana, is revolutionizing football development in Southern Africa. Founded in 2025, we've established ourselves as the premier destination for aspiring strikers, with a proven track record of developing players who compete at national and international levels.

Our academy was born from a vision to transform raw talent from Botswana's township communities into professional footballers. We believe in empowering youth through sport, providing opportunities that extend beyond the football pitch.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and develop exceptional striking talent from underserved communities, providing world-class training and life skills for success on and off the pitch.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading striker development academy, producing players who compete at the highest levels globally while positively impacting their communities.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our academy

Discipline

We instill professional habits, punctuality, and respect - the foundation of athletic excellence.

Passion

We cultivate love for the game and relentless pursuit of improvement in every player.

Community

We believe in giving back and uplifting our communities through football development.
